Odyssey is a Mega Event organized by St. Joseph’s School, Greater Noida. It is a vibrant inter-school fest that brings together talent, creativity, and innovation on a single platform.



Students participate in a wide range of cultural, literary, artistic, and technical competitions. The event encourages teamwork, leadership, and confidence while promoting healthy competition. Odyssey is not just a contest, but a celebration of learning, collaboration, and youthful energy.



The event also serves as a bridge between knowledge and expression, allowing students to test their potential in real-world challenges. From thought-provoking debates to mesmerizing performances, every activity at Odyssey aims to nurture confidence and leadership in young learners.




Odyssey reflects the vision of St. Joseph’s School to encourage holistic development and excellence among students. It is not just an event, but a journey that celebrates talent, strengthens bonds, and inspires future leaders.
